WHAT IS KEEP GREEN

Recycle with Rewards

Collect rewards in $GREEN tokens when you recycle aluminum cans at Keep Green locations.

Gamification

Compete with friends, track your progress on the leaderboard, and achieve individual and collective recycling goals.

Tokenized Economy

Use your $GREEN tokens within the Keep Green ecosystem, invest in DeFi projects on the Solana blockchain, or convert them to fiat currency. The choice is yours!

OUR MISSION

Keep green is a simple and innovative solution that promotes both recycling and cryptocurrency adoption. All this through a friendly and accessible rewards system on Solana.

We will decentralize collection points for recyclables and increase the utility and adoption of Solana, while encouraging sustainable practices.

OUR PILLARS

Increase recycling rates, reduce waste, and promote the reuse of materials. Social: Build a community of users dedicated to sustainable living and environmental.
